My Journey:

My name is Edward Wang.

I started learning tennis in the summer of 2021.

In the beginning, I didn't really like it since I had to wake up every day at 7 to catch my morning lesson.

However, it is now one of my favorite hobbies, and I enjoy playing it with my friends.

One day, I hope to make my high school's varsity tennis team.




My Tennis Idol:

My favorite tennis player is Roger Federer, who is the first person to win 20 grand slams in tennis.

 

Roger Federer has been number one in the world for 310 weeks.

My favorite tennis tournament is Wimbledon, which is held in London every year.

Roger Federer has won 8 Wimbledon titles.

Roger Federer's biggest rivals were Rafael Nadal and Novak Djokovic.

Roger Federer retired from tennis in September 2022. Federer's last match was the Laver Cup, where he played doubles with Rafael Nadal. Although he didn't win, it was a very emotional game for him.

Federer has picked up the nickname "the maestro". He is an icon of the sport and often associated with elegance and graciousness.
